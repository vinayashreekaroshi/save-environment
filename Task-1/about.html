Know more about us

Hello, we are Save Our Environment, a little bit of doing our bit to make the planet a better and healthier place to live in. think that nature protection does not only show that we have a duty but actually express gratitude to the Earth who provides us with all. This is how we began with an idea that was simple, what in case every single person did something every day to save the nature? That thought produced a mission, a way to make people think cares, act and make a real change. We are an environmental organization called Save Our Environment and we aim at creating awareness of the environment in terms of climate change, pollution, deforestation, and the reduction of waste. We strive to ensure that sustainability becomes the way of life of every person through little efforts, community programs, and daily activities.

Our vision

To create the world in which people and nature can exist in harmony where clean air, clean water, and green environment are the elements of human life.

Our Mission

To inspire individuals to make small but significant changes that would produce a large difference, such as planting trees and reducing plastic as well as saving energy and raising awareness.
